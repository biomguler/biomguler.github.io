About the i-CLASSi Tool

Disclaimer: This tool is developed for research and educational purposes only. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis or treatment guidance.

Overview

The InterLymph Consortium (International Consortium of Investigators Working on Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma Epidemiologic Studies) is an open scientific forum for epidemiologic research in non-Hodgkin's lymphoma. Formed in 2001, the Consortium is a group of international investigators who collaborate on case–control studies and pooled data analyses, advancing lymphoma epidemiology through standardized classification and collaborative research.

Learn more at the InterLymph website.

Pathology Working Group

The InterLymph Pathology Working Group includes hematopathologists, epidemiologists, and researchers committed to harmonizing lymphoma disease classification for epidemiologic studies.
